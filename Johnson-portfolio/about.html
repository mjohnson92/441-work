Michael Johnson

Michael Johnson

Student

Age 25

Michael is a media artist, graphic designer, and an experimental musician. He is studying to achieve an online bachelor's degree in media arts at the University of Montana in Missoula, Montana. While he has studied, his passion has grown towards the field of graphic design. He is interested in expressing his vision through minimalist, futuristic, and modernistic designs for album artwork, advertisements, and layouts for websites. In Michael's spare time, he works on experimental music that ranges from power ambient to techno. Looking toward the future, Michael plans to continue to work on music, his designs, and wants to begin working on illustrations and typography.
