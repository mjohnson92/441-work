Garden of Layla

Audio by Meta

Label

Self Released on Bandcamp

Date

21 March 2018

Genre

Power Ambient / Trance

Ecophagy: The consuming of ecosystems by nanotechnology that has gone awry. Enter the Garden of Layla, where ecophagy has overtaken the landscape in its entirety. Wherein the LAYLA machines have displayed unrelenting control and manipulation of the once pure and organic inhabitants. Rose and cobalt luminescence dance and taunt in waves, a byproduct of the ruthless process. Venture into the biological desecration. Garden of Layla is a sound excursion that explores the reign of sentient technology over nature. Inspired by sound designed-noise and millenial trance, two expressive versions emerge showing diverse locations and events inside the garden. The listener experiences the trek through the storm, direct contact with the violent LAYLA systems, and even a glimpse at their mysterious AI technology.
