ABOUT

Michael is a media artist, graphic designer, and an experimental musician. He is studying to achieve an online bachelor's degree in media arts at the University of Montana in Missoula, Montana. While he has studied, his passion has grown towards the field of graphic design. He is interested in expressing his vision through minimalist, futuristic, and modernistic designs for album artwork, advertisements, and layouts for websites. In Michael's spare time, he works on experimental music that ranges from power ambient to techno. Looking toward the future, Michael plans to continue to work on music, his designs, and wants to begin working on illustrations and typography.

RECENT PROJECTS

Meta -
Garden of Layla
Garden of Layla is the result of a small project focus on the concept of ecophagy. Ecophagy is described as an epidemic of out of control self-replicating nanorobots that consume entire ecosystems. Two tracks, inspired by the terrors of what the world would be like if we experienced this situation. Two different versions of the same track, one having a more natural feel and the other fused with the energy of modern trance. The music from this ep is not yet released.

Meta -
Eva (010)
Eva is a track for the compilation "010" for RE:VIVE and the record label Fog Mountain. 14 artists were asked to create an original sonic piece with only archival recordings from the streets of Rotterdam Netherlands in the 40's. Re:vive is an initiative to reuse archival recordings to create new music through the sounds of the past.

Meta’s “Eva" is a dense, futuristic matrix of industrial noise. - Holly Dicker, Resident Advisor
