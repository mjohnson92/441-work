Welcome to your adventure!

You are hiking and suddenly you realize something.
You are lost on a mountainside and looking for your way back home.
You can choose to either go east or west. Which way will you go?

You've headed East

You find yourself at the edge of a lake. It's beauty keeps you in awe, you decide to rest.
You catch some fish and cook them by a fire. You wake up bright and early the next morning.
You start to question the route you started to take. where will you go next?

You've Headed West

You find yourself on a cliff looking at a beautiful landscape.
You watch as the sun sets on the horizon. You have good faith you're on the right path.
You see a cave down low in the distance. Maybe it's a shortcut. Which way will you go?

Death Was Inevitable

You search for a way through the cave but trip and fall.
You fear you've made a terrible mistake.
You fall down a hole a crack your skull open!

You've reached a Valley

It's a beautiful place to rest. There may be a good spot up ahead.
You wander through the valley and spot smoke over the hill.
Theres a chance it might be civilization, do you keep going?

You've found your home!

You sprint down to your front door with joy.
It's time to kick off the shoes and take a nap! You feel accomplished.
